Page Details & Purpose

Page Primary Goal Key Content Sections Tags
Home First impression — communicate trust, clarity and the pentagon planning framework Hero with bold headline, five-point services overview (pentagon motif), brief values statement, social proof (years of experience, client satisfaction), CTA to Services and Contact LandingCTA
About Us Build credibility and a human connection with prospective clients Firm founding story, adviser profiles with qualifications, FCA authorisation and regulatory credentials, firm values aligned to client-first philosophy TrustCredentials
Services Clearly explain the five service pillars and drive enquiries IHT Planning (estate & legacy structuring), Investment Management (portfolio strategy), Retirement Planning (lifestyle & income goals), Pension Management (consolidation, drawdown, annuity), Insurance & Protection (life, critical illness, income protection) — each with a dedicated sub-section and "Find out more" CTA Core5 Pillars
Privacy Policy Legal compliance — GDPR / UK Data Protection Act transparency Data collection & processing, cookies and tracking, lawful basis for processing, client rights (access, erasure, portability), contact details for DPO / complaints LegalGDPR
Contact Us Convert visitors into enquiries — low barrier to reach the team Simple enquiry form (name, email, service interest, message), direct phone and email, office address, optional appointment scheduler (e.g. Calendly embed) ConversionCTA
